Who We Are

Located in the vibrant city of Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a premier boutique hair salon that caters to those seeking exceptional cut, color, and styling services. With a distinct focus on innovation and creativity, our salon has become synonymous with edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts that allow our clients to express their unique style.


Beyond the salon experience, Hair Day strives to be a source of inspiration and education for our clients. We believe that beauty goes beyond the chair, which is why our team regularly shares their expertise through makeup and nail tutorials featured on our website. We aim to empower you with the knowledge and tools to continue looking and feeling fabulous between appointments.

Meet The Owners

At Hair Day, we understand that your hair is a canvas waiting to be transformed, and our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to bringing your vision to life. Led by our passionate owner, Pria, who holds a coveted certificate as a colorist, our salon has built a reputation for delivering breathtaking and personalized color techniques. Pria's unwavering commitment to her craft has made her an authority in the industry, and her expertise sets the foundation for the exceptional service we provide.

Collaborating with Pria is Garry, an accomplished and talented hairdresser who shares the same passion for creating stunning looks. Together, they have meticulously handpicked a team of seven highly skilled individuals who are equally passionate about their craft. Our staff includes two masterful stylists, three brilliant colorists, a meticulous manicurist, and a skilled make-up artist. Each member of our team brings a unique blend of talent, creativity, and professionalism to ensure that every visit to Hair Day is an extraordinary experience.
